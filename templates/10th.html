Indian Army Soldier Recruitment


Indian army conducts soldier recruitment for various categories like Soldier General Duty, Soldier Technical, Soldier Clerk/Storekeeper Technical, etc., can be applied for after class 10.


Eligibility: The age limit varies from one post to the other In general, class 10th /matric pass with min 45% mks in aggregate and min 33% in each subject.

Official Website: Army Recruitment

Tamil Nadu Combined Civil Services Examination (Group 4)


Brief Detail: The TNPSC Group 4 examination is conducted for the recruitment of candidates to various positions such as Junior Assistant, Typist, Stenographer, Village Administrative Officer (VAO), Field Surveyor, and Draftsman in the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service, Tamil Nadu Judicial Ministerial Service, Tamil Nadu Survey and Land Records Subordinate Service, etc.

Eligibility: Age Limit: The minimum age limit is 18 years, and the maximum age limit varies according to the category of the candidate. Educational Qualification: Candidates should have completed their 10th standard or equivalent qualification from a recognized board or institution.

Official Website : TNPSC-Group 4

Indian Navy Tradesman Mate Recruitment Examination


Brief Detail: The Indian Navy conducts recruitment examinations to fill vacancies for the post of Tradesman Mate in various Naval Commands. Tradesman Mates are responsible for performing skilled and semi-skilled tasks related to trades such as carpentry, plumbing, welding, electrical work, and general maintenance onboard ships and naval establishments.

Eligibility: Age Limit: Candidates must be between 18 to 25 years of age. Age relaxation is applicable for candidates belonging to reserved categories as per government norms. Educational Qualification: Candidates should have completed their 10th standard (Matriculation) from a recognized board or institution.

Official Website : INDIAN NAVY TRADESMAN

Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) - Group D


Brief Detail: RRB Group D exam is for various posts like Track Maintainer, Helper, and Assistant in Indian Railways.

Eligibility: 10th pass or ITI

Official Website : RRB-Group D

Railway Protection Force (RPF) Constable Recruitment


Brief Detail: Railway Protection Force (RPF) Constables are responsible for maintaining law and order within the railway premises, ensuring the safety and security of passengers and railway property. They play a crucial role in preventing crimes, ensuring the safety of passengers, and enforcing regulations.

Age Limit: Candidates must be between 18 to 25 years of age. Age relaxation is applicable for candidates belonging to reserved categories as per government norms. Educational Qualification: Candidates should have completed their 10th standard (Matriculation) from a recognized board or institution.10th pass or ITI

Official Website : RPF
