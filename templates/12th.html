Staff Selection Commission


Staff Selection Commission Combined Higher Secondary Level (SSC CHSL) Staff Selection Commission conducts a competitive Combined Higher Secondary Level examination for various posts in the different offices, departments, and ministries of the Government of India. The posts that the candidates can get after clearing these exams are:

  • Data Entry Operator (DEO)
  • Sorting Assistant (SA) / Postal Assistant (PA)
  • Secretariat Assistant (JSA) / Lower Divisional Clerk (LDC)


    • Eligibility: The age limit varies from one post to the other In general, class 10th /matric pass with min 45% mks in aggregate and min 33% in each subject.

    Official Website : SSC

    Railway Recruitment


    Railway Recruitment Board Exams (RRB Examination) RRB conducts Computer Based Test (CBT) for several posts. The posts conducted for these exams are:

  • Junior Clerk-cum-Typist
  • Accounts Clerk-cum-Typist
  • Trains Clerk


    • Eligibility: The age limit varies from one post to the other In general, the limit of the age of every candidate is from 18 years to 30 years

    Official Website : RRB

    Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) - Group D


    Brief Detail: RRB Group D exam is for various posts like Track Maintainer, Helper, and Assistant in Indian Railways.


    Eligibility: 10+2 or equivalent


    Official Website : RRB-Group D

    Naval Academy


    Naval Academy and National Defense Academy Examinations (NA & NDA Examination)


    Eligibility: The limit of age is from 17 years to 20 years Candidates can appear for these government competitive exams after 12th from any stream

    Official Website : Naval Academy

    Indian Navy Sailor


    The Indian Navy selects candidates when they complete government competitive exams after 12th successfully as sailors. The posts that they get after clearing these exams are: Seaman after training Artificer apprentice in various branches.


    Eligibility: The limit of age is from 17 years to 20 years

    Central Industrial Security Force (CISF) Constable/Tradesman Recruitment


    Brief Detail: CISF recruits Constable/Tradesman for various trades.

    Eligibility: The limit of age is from 18 years to 28 years There is relaxation of age limit as well for candidates belonging to various categories

    Official Website : CISF

    Railway Group


    The RRB conducts this exam to recruit candidates for various posts like Track Maintainer Grade-IV and Assistant/Helper Pointsman in various departments of Indian Railways. Candidates can go through RRB Group D notification for the latest vacancies and updates.


    Eligibility: The limit of age is from 18 years to 33 years

    Official Website : Railway Group

    Railway Constables


    Brief Detail: The Railway Constables maintain the right order and law in the railway premises. Candidates who have applied for the government competitive exams after 12th can apply for this post.

    Eligibility: The limit of age is from 18 years to 25 years


    Official Website : Railway Constables

    National Defence Academy (NDA) Examination


    Brief Detail: The NDA Examination is conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) for admission to the Army, Navy, and Air Force wings of the National Defence Academy (NDA) and Indian Naval Academy (INA). Successful candidates are admitted to the respective academies for training to become officers in the Indian Armed Forces.

    Eligibility: Age Limit: Candidates must be between 16.5 to 19.5 years of age. Age relaxation is applicable for candidates belonging to reserved categories as per government norms. Educational Qualification: Candidates should have completed their 12th standard (Intermediate) from a recognized board or institution.


    Official Website : NDA
