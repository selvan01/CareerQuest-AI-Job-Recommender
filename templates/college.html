For Graduate Students

Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) Civil Services Exam


Description: The UPSC Civil Services Exam is one of the most prestigious exams for recruitment to various civil services including IAS, IPS, and IFS.


Eligibility: A degree in any discipline from a recognized university.

Official Website: UPSC

Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE)


Description: GATE is conducted jointly by the IISc and seven IITs for admission into postgraduate engineering programs and recruitment to various public sector jobs.


Eligibility: Bachelor's degree in Engineering/Technology or Master's degree in Science.

Official Website : GATE

Staff Selection Commission Combined Graduate Level (SSC CGL)


Description: SSC CGL is conducted for recruitment to various Group B and C posts in government departments and ministries.



Eligibility: Bachelor's degree from a recognized university.

Official Website : SSC CGL

Reserve Bank of India (RBI) Grade B Officer Exam


Description: RBI Grade B Exam recruits officers for various positions in the Reserve Bank of India.



Eligibility: A minimum of 60% marks in Bachelor’s degree.

Official Website : RBI-GRADE B

State Public Service Commission Exams (State-wise)


Description: State PSCs conduct exams for various administrative and civil services positions in the respective states.



Eligibility: Varies by state, generally a Bachelor's degree.

Official Website : Varies by state

SBI Probationary Officer (PO) Exam


Description: SBI PO Exam recruits Probationary Officers for the State Bank of India.



Eligibility: A degree in any discipline from a recognized university.

Official Website: SBI PO EXAM

Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI) Exam


Description: IRDAI conducts exams for the recruitment of various positions in the insurance sector.


Eligibility: Varies by position, generally a Bachelor’s degree.

Official Website : IRDAI EXAM

National Eligibility Test (NET) for Lectureship and Junior Research Fellowship


Description: NET is conducted for determining eligibility for the post of Assistant Professor or Junior Research Fellowship in Indian universities and colleges.


Eligibility: Master's degree or equivalent with 55% marks.

Official Website :NET EXAM

Indian Statistical Service (ISS) Exam


Description: ISS Exam is conducted by UPSC for the recruitment of statisticians in various government departments.


Eligibility: Bachelor's degree with Statistics/Mathematical Statistics/Applied Statistics as one of the subjects.

Official Website : ISS EXAM

Indian Economic Service (IES) Exam


Description: IES Exam is conducted by UPSC for the recruitment of economists in various government departments.



Eligibility: Post-graduate degree in Economics/Applied Economics/Business Economics/Econometrics.

Official Website : IES Exam
